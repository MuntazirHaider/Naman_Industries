How long does sea freight take?

Typical transit times vary depending on origin and shipping route usually between 20 to 40 days from port departure to arrival. Delays depend on shipping route, customs clearance and inland transport.

What documents are needed for sea freight import?

We handle commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, certificate of origin (if required), and customs documentation you dont have to worry about paperwork.

Can I track my shipment?

Yes we provide regular updates including container number, vessel status, ETA and port details.

Do you handle customs clearance and duties?

Yes our team manages full customs clearance in India, pays applicable duties/taxes, and handles all required formalities on your behalf.

Do you offer door-to-door delivery?

Yes we offer port-to-port and door-to-door solutions, delivering your shipment to your warehouse or address in India.
